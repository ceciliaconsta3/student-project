Family

Born and raised on the beaches of South Florida, like my name suggests, I'm a copper-skinned Hispanic of Irish descent. I own an adorable three year old cockatiel by the name of Oliver, have a handful of male siblings (all taller than me somehow?!), two young and sexy parents, and a great fiance.

Work and Education

I initially started my career with two degrees in Biological Science and went to work for biopharmaceutical company a few days before finishing. After speaking with, shadowing, and interacting with co-workers from different departments, volunteering for trainings, and receiving a promotion, I decided I wanted to venture outside the lab for a career that could challenge and captivate me for a lifetime.

After attending a hackathon (moral support was all I could provide you see), diving through a six month program at a vocational institute, and interviewing with countless web designers, developers, and a couple of engineers, I knew what I wanted. I got my MTA, became a humble intern, and enrolled for the Spring term, ready and eager to struggle, learn, and participate with the best of them.
